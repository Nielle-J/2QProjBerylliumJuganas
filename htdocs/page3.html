Toppings Guide

As you progress into the game, you might find these topping slots beside your cookie in their roster. These toppings help your cookie become stronger in different ways. There are 10 toppings, you could boost your cookie's attack, attack speed, damage resist, crit resist, crit rate, defense, hp, debuff resist, or amplify buff or cooldown.

You can also level these toppings up, which gives extra substats to your toppings! Below is a list of toppings that you can use, and which cookies are best suited for each topping. Note that some cookies need a combination of two toppings; those cookies will be seen in the list of both toppings.


Searing Raspberry

Searing Raspberry boosts your cookie's attack. This is the most used topping by cookies, but acquiring this type of topping might be a little difficult due to how rare it is.

This topping is best suited for these cookies: Espresso, Poision Mushroom, Herb, Parfait, Pomegranate, Sorbet Shark, Black Raisin, Vampire, Chili Pepper, Pastry, Rye, Squid Ink, Mango, Latte, Mala Sauce, Purple Yam and Pumpkin Pie.



Solid Almond

Solid Almond boosts your cookie's damage resistance. This is the usual topping used by tanks and defense cookies.

This topping is best suited for these cookies: Pure Vanilla, Hollyberry, Moon Rabbit, Strawberry Crepe, Madeleine, Milk, Mala Sauce, Red Velvet, Kumiho, Purple Yam, Dark Choco, Werewolf, Tea Knight, Cocoa and Pumpkin Pie.



Swift Chocolate

Swift Choco decreases your cookie's cooldown time. This is the usual topping used by DPS cookies with long cooldowns.

This topping is best suited for these cookies: Pure Vanilla, Hollyberry, Sea Fairy, Parfait, Sonic, Sparkling, Cream Puff, Fig, Lilac, Pomegranate, Mint Choco, Almond, Tiger Lily, Mango, Latte, Licorice, Snow Sugar, Moon Rabbit, Raspberry, Cotton, Frost Queen and Eclair.



Juicy Apple Jelly

Apple Jelly boosts your cookie's crit rate. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is best suited for this cookie: Twizzly Gummy.




Bouncy Caramel

Bouncy Caramel boosts your cookie's attack speed. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is not recommended for any cookie; however, you may use it on your own cookies.




Healthy Peanut

Healthy Peanut increases your cookie's HP. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is not recommended for any cookie; however, you may use it on your own cookies, especially ones with low health.



Hard Walnut

Hard Walnut increases your cookie's defesne. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is not recommended for any cookie; however, you may use it on your own cookies, especially for tanks as a substitute for solid almond.



Sweet Candy

Sweet Candy amplifies your cookie's buffs. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is not recommended for any cookie; however, you may use it on your own cookies.




Hearty Hazelnut

Hearty Hazelnut increases your cookie's crit resist. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is not recommended for any cookie; however, you may use it on your own cookies.




Fresh Kiwi

Fresh Kiwi increases your cookie's debuff effects. This is not usually used, as the first three are the most effective toppings.

This topping is not recommended for any cookie; however, you may use it on your own cookies.
